PR: Persistent Contact Manifolds (Feature-ID Warm-Start) We will upgrade the contact solver in kineticContactManifold.js and kineticContactSolver.js from a simple spatial decay cache to true feature-id warm-starting. Currently, contactWarmStartKey just quantizes the contact normal angle. We will change this to incorporate actual geometric feature IDs returned by the SAT narrow phase (e.g., the specific vertex index of polygon A hitting the specific edge index of polygon B). By caching the exact normal (jn) and tangent (jt) impulses against these stable feature IDs, the solver can perfectly recall the exact forces needed to keep bodies separated across frames. This provides textbook Box2D-level stacking stability, drastically reducing the iterations needed in solveKineticContactVelocities and completely eliminating jitter when the 100 snakes stack, coil, or pile up against walls.
